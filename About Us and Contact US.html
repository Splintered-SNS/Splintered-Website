About Us

Splintered is a unique take on mental health. This website aims to educate and erase the stigma surrounding mental health, consequently promoting self-expression. Mental health is an unspoken issue that silently affects nearly 1 in 4 people globally.


A study by the WHO states that at least 6.5% of the Indian population suffers from some form of serious mental disorder. Due to the stigma associated with mental illness, a lack of awareness, and limited access to professional help, only 10-12% of these sufferers will seek help.


This website consists of information about some of the most impactful mental disorders, to help the community recognize symptoms. The site provides a platform for concerned or sympathetic people to volunteer and support at-risk individuals. It encourages people to speak up and voice their struggles through the anonymous sharing of stories.
